Pronouns

pronouns are one of the main staples of the language

the most important ideas to remember about pronouns are:

  • pronouns can be singular or plural

  • pronouns can be objective or subjective

  • pronouns refer back to a specific noun*

but what does all that mean? let's try and break that down

singular or plural

whether something is singular or plural all depends on the count but it's easy

singular is one or none, never any more

that's 1 or 0.

you may be asking how is 0 singular? Well I'm asking that question too
